Unlike other job marketplace sites, Jott.ly focuses on editing projects. With 30,000+ editors across the globe and integrated collaboration and project management tools, Jott.ly's a one-stop-shop for your editing needs.

See how it works

Built-in collaboration space

Creativity and good work doesn't happen in a vacuum. Writers and editors can collaborate in a dedicated project space. With document version history, commenting, and multi-user editing, writers can actively participate in the editing process.


Editor profiles

Pick the perfect editor for your writing project. Profiles let you see each each freelance editor's experience with different style guides, industries, and types of content. You can even see how editors have been rated for past Jott.ly jobs.


Project milestones

Keep your editing project on track by setting milestones. You can even opt to make payments for work based on the meeting of milestones. Writers pay only for the work that's been done and freelancers reduce their risk of working for free.


Integrated calendar

Make sure everyone's on the same page with project calendars. Calendars automatically display project milestones, and you mark additional events like task due dates or meetings.
